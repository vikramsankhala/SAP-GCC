Solutioning

SAP BI Kickoff for Pharma GCC

A practical, phased approach to SAP implementation and SAP BI for US pharma GCCs in India

The Pharma GCC SAP Landscape

A US pharma company setting up a GCC in India typically needs to both implement the core SAP ERP backbone and simultaneously stand up a BI and analytics capability that serves US-side commercial, medical, and operations teams. These two tracks run in parallel and must be coordinated from day one.

Phased Implementation Approach

SAP implementation for a pharma GCC is best structured in four phases that move from foundation to production analytics to AI augmentation. Each phase delivers standalone business value while building on the previous.

Data Architecture & Foundations

Kicking off SAP BI correctly means getting the data architecture right before building any dashboards. A rushed BI layer built on top of raw transactional tables is the single most common failure mode in pharma analytics programs.

The Three-Layer Pharma BI Architecture ensures clean, governed data products before any dashboards are built.

Use Cases

Commercial & Medical Analytics

Highest-priority BI deliverables – each maps to SAP Analytics Cloud or Power BI, built on Datasphere

01

Brand Performance Dashboard

The foundational commercial analytics report. Tracks how a drug brand is performing across geographies, time periods, and channels.

02

Sales Force Effectiveness (SFE)

Measures how productively each sales representative and MSL deploys time against the right HCPs – the highest-ROI question in pharma commercial operations.

03

HCP Segmentation & Profiling

Identifies and ranks Healthcare Professionals by potential value, engagement level, and receptivity – the strategic core of pharma commercial planning.

04

Patient Funnel & Access Analytics

Tracks the patient journey from diagnosis through prescribing, dispensing, and adherence – with drop-off analysis at each stage.

05

Campaign & Marketing Analytics

Measures effectiveness of DTC, digital, and HCP-directed marketing campaigns on brand and Rx outcomes.

06

Medical Affairs – MSL & KOL Engagement

KOL mapping (influence network from publications, speaker events, advisory boards), MSL territory analytics, clinical trial site identification, and Medical Information Request (MIR) analytics.

07

Supply Chain & Operations

Demand sensing: S/4HANA IBP analytics in SAC – comparing statistical forecast to consensus demand plan to actuals, with exception-based alerts.
